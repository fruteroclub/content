The community opened applications for **cohort 3** of its mentorship program: 40 seats,
applications through June 30. Priority goes to builders who have already shipped something
verifiable, not to those just starting to explore.

The program pairs each participant with a mentor for six weeks, around a concrete goal set
on day one and reviewed in public at the end. It is not a course: it is real work on your
own project, with someone who has already walked the path.
