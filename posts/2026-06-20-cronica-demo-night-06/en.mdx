The sixth Demo Night filled the room in Mexico City: 219 people who came to see what
the community had been building this month. No investor pitch, no promises — just
people showing what already works.

Three teams presented live. The first, a verifiable reputation panel for mentors;
the second, a contract-review tool; the third, an onboarding flow that brings a first
deploy down to a single afternoon.

The best part happened after the demos, in the hallways: introductions, questions
answered, and at least two collaborations that kicked off that same night.
